About Me

Welcome to my website. My name is David Sung and I'm a UC Berkeley graduate studying Computer Science and Cognitive Science with a focus on User Experience and Web Development.


I started this page as a personal project to hone my Web Developing skills, something I got interested way back in middle-school toying around with MySpace layouts. This website was initially created from scratch using entirely HTML/CSS and custom JavaScript/jQuery functions to animate elements on the page. To familiarize myself with different JavaScript Frameworks and play around with some Google APIs, I decided to integrate the BootStrap framework into the website to clean up a some of the code along with the design. Feel free to check out some of my other projects in the menu.


Regarding my professional experience, I had the chance to work as a Web Developer for the Cal Alumni Association at UC Berkeley for over a year under the Grad Division Department where I teamed with other back-end developers and designers to revamp the website. I also had the chance to intern at a small Information Technology start-up in Santa Monica as a Front-End Web Development intern last summer where I mainly worked with HTML/CSS and JavaScript to create data web-based reports for schools in the Bay area. With my passion for design and the web, I plan on working a couple years as a Front-End Web Developer as I transition into the Full-Stack Developer role once I am more knowledgeable of the back-end part of the web. I am constantly looking for new ways to improve; if you'd like to get in touch regarding any professional opportunities in front-end, UI/UX Design, my contact information can be found in the dropdown menu!
